Popular Aesthetics

Artsy

People with this aesthetic tend to like art(hence the name), yellow, mom jeans, sandals, and of course, the kanken rucksack. It is also typical for them to have Van Gogh socks.

Baddie

Well, its not. At least to the everyday person. However, it can play a huge part in someone's brand online, and help them create a loyal following of people who share similar tastes.
